Our Story

A Tournament Built on Legacy

The Justin Bourassa Memorial Tennis Tournament is a celebration of tennis, community, and the enduring legacy of Justin Bourassa. Built in his memory, the event brings together players from across Canada and the US for an unforgettable week of matches, sportsmanship, and connection.

There will be Open 1000 level draws sanctioned by the OTA, along with Club level draws for anyone wanting to compete. There will also be doubles draws, along with a brand new One-Point Tournament open to everyone.

Beyond the courts, the tournament welcomes fans, volunteers, and sponsors who help make the experience exceptional - featuring player amenities, a full event schedule, and a vibrant on-site atmosphere. Join us to compete, support, and honour Justin through the sport he loved.

The Love All: Justin Bourassa Foundation was created to honour Justin's memory and keep his spirit alive in the community he loved. Through initiatives like this tournament, the Foundation supports youth tennis development, community programs, and the values of sportsmanship and connection that Justin embodied. The silent auction held each year directly benefits the Foundation and its ongoing work.
